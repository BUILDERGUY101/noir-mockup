Our Mission

We saw a problem. Small business owners, freelancers, and startups are passionate and brilliant, but they often get stuck. Building a professional website or app is a mountain of time, money, and technical headaches. Many can't afford developers, and the existing "DIY" tools are often restrictive and still require a ton of manual work.

That's why we created NØIR. We believe that leveraging the true power of generative AI can level the playing field. Our goal is to provide a service that is not just affordable, but genuinely autonomous. We want to empower creators to bring their vision to life without being bogged down by the process.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to know how to code?

Absolutely not! That's the whole point of NØIR. You just need an idea. You communicate with our AI in natural language, and it handles all the coding and design for you.

Is my website truly unique?

Yes. We do not use templates. Every single website is generated from the ground up by our AI based on your specific inputs and goals. Your site will be one-of-a-kind.

How is this different from Wix or Durable?

The key difference is autonomy. While other builders assist you, NØIR does the work for you. They require you to manually edit and place elements. With NØIR, you can simply tell the AI, "Change the color of the buttons to blue," and it's done. We save you time and creative energy.

Can I use my own domain name?

Yes! Our Starter plan and above allow you to connect your own custom domain. Our Business and Professional plans even include a free domain for the first year.
